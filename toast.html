Animation Implementation

Each position now has its own animation direction:

  • Right positions - Slide in from right, slide out to right
  • Left positions - Slide in from left, slide out to left
  • Top center - Slide down from top, slide up to top
  • Bottom center - Slide up from bottom, slide down to bottom
  • Auto-stacking - Multiple toasts stack with proper animations
  • Smooth transitions - All animations use CSS transitions
